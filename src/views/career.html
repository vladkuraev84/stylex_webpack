Marketing

  • You will be the owner of marketing initiatives

  • You are a true owner that sees opportunities and actions on them
  • You have experience in content marketing, campaign development, acquisition & engagement channels
  • Project management: you have the ability to manage multiple projects at scale, while working with the main team and external third parties as required
